About Alagoas

Alagoas is a state in northeastern Brazil known for its stunning beaches, rich culture, and vibrant tourism. The state boasts some of the most beautiful beaches in the country, with crystal-clear waters and golden sands that attract visitors from all over the world. Alagoas is also renowned for its rich cultural heritage, with a blend of African, indigenous, and Portuguese influences that can be seen in its music, dance, and cuisine.

Demographics

Population: 3,337,357 (2021) 📊

Area: 27,767 km² 🌎

Capital: Maceió 🌆

Statistics

Alagoas has an average annual temperature of 25°C. 🌡️

The state is known for its diverse flora and fauna. 🌿🐦

Alagoas ranks among the top Brazilian states for coconut and sugarcane production. 🥥🌾
